Before addressing about our event, we want you to know what WiOS is

Women in Open Source, VIT-AP is a non-profitable chapter at VIT-AP. Our goal is to bring gender diversity in technology alongside encourage everyone to step in and contribute to the open-source communities. We organize events and hackathons to bring about awareness of open-source technologies in our university.
